Overview of SONY Corporation

Sony Corporation (ソニー株式会社 (Sonī kabushiki gaisha) commonly known as Sony and stylized as SONY) is a Japanese multinational conglomerate corporation headquartered in Kōnan, Minato, Tokyo. The company operates as one of the world's largest manufacturers of consumer and professional electronic products, the largest video game console company and the second largest video game publisher, as well as one of the most comprehensive media companies.
